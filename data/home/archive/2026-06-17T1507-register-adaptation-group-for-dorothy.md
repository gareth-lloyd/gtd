Dear Parents,

You are receiving this email because you have registered your child for the first time at one of Dorothy Snot's schools for the upcoming 2026-27 school year and we thank you for that!

By clicking here you can tell us within what period you would like your child's adaptation to begin.

Fill in all the weeks during which it would be convenient for you to begin the adaptation.

In the same form you can also fill in up to 5 names of children who you may know are attending our school and who you would like to be in the same class (obviously what you ask us is not binding, but it will help us in the better organization of the school for the upcoming school year).

Fill in the form if you wish by 30/6/2026.

In the coming weeks, we will inform you through classdojo about the date of the first meeting at the school, just for you, the new parents, in July.

The meetings will be separate for each age and you will be able to come if you want with your child, to meet the teachers of your classes in person and to discuss the adjustment and anything else that concerns you.
